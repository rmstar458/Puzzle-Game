Interactive Puzzle

Do you know what are soft skills ? Soft skills are a combination of personal attributes, social and communication abilities, and emotional intelligence that enable individuals to interact effectively with others, work well in teams, and accomplish their goals.

So we develop a simple theme based game. It uses the soft skills of a person to solve certain problems like : General Intelligence,Communications skills,Curiosity,Creativity etc.



Here are some rule you should keep in mind before starting the game.


  1. There are certain levels you need to pass the current level to go to next level.
  2. There is no Time Limit.
  3. You are also Provided with the hints if you got stuck somewhere. Carefully read and observe the hints as the answer hides in them.
  4. There are certain DeadEnd in the game. It means if you proceed with the wrong answer than you will be in a DeadEnd state from where you cann't escape and the Game ends there.
  5. Observe and Remember each and everthing you notice duirng the Game as it will be helpful at every level in the Game.

Let's Find the Eldorado Treasure

You are an adventurer on a quest to find the lost treasure of the ancient city of Eldorado. Legend has said that the treasure is hidden somewhere in the city, but the only clue to its location is a riddle that has been passed down through generations:


"From the great city gate, take the western road,

Past the old stone fountain and the farmer's abode.

Turn right at the statue of the warrior bold,

And follow the path where the trees are old.

Look for the sign of the serpent's head,

And there the treasure shall be found, it is said."


So as you follow the path, at every point you have to find some hidden code to proceed to the next level. So try your best to break all the puzzles and find the lost treasure of Eldorado. Make Sure you read the statement carefully because once started there is no coming back.
